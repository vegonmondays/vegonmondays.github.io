Cacophony of noises

12 September 2025

The city hummed beneath the surface, a low drone of engines and far-off chatter, punctuated by a kettle’s whistle and a dog’s bark.

Inside her head, sounds overlapped and tangled—half-formed thoughts, restless questions, the steady tick of the wall clock.

She watched the light shift across the room and tried to separate one sound from another, as if pulling threads from a knot.
